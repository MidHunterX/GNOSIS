Viewer/Uploader Modes split selection.

The split selection screen allows users to seamlessly switch between Viewer and Uploader modes. In Viewer mode, users can browse and consume content, while in Uploader mode, they can contribute new content to the platform. This feature enhances user experience by providing quick and easy access to both modes, making it convenient to consume and contribute content.

Generative AI Answers for searches.

Our Generative AI technology enables the platform to provide intelligent and context-aware answers to user queries. This feature goes beyond simple keyword matching, allowing the system to understand the intent behind the question and generate detailed and relevant answers. It enhances the user experience by providing accurate and informative responses tailored to individual queries.

More options for Multimedia answers.

The platform supports a wide range of multimedia formats, including video, audio, images, and documents, to provide comprehensive answers. This feature enriches the user experience by offering multimedia content that complements text-based answers, making it easier for users to understand complex topics and concepts.

Ask questions to community.

Users can engage with the platform's community by asking questions and receiving answers from other members. This feature fosters knowledge sharing and collaboration, allowing users to tap into the collective expertise of the community. It encourages active participation and helps users gain insights and perspectives from diverse viewpoints.

Give suggestions to answers.

Users can contribute to the platform by providing suggestions to existing answers. This feature allows users to offer feedback on the accuracy, relevance, and clarity of answers, helping to improve the overall quality of content.
